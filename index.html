Sunday League football team based in Hemel Hempstead

Intro to club

We are the Rose and Crown FC; a fun, friendly group of guys who play in the Berkhamstead Sunday league. We are based in Hemel Hempstead and we train twice a week on weekday evenings and play competitive matches against other local teams every Sunday morning. This club's ethos is all about having fun and providing an opportunity to socialise with other people and play the game we all love.

Reasons to join

Fitness and health

With the training sessions and the mathes, there is plenty of running involved which is a great way to increase your football skills and overall fitness

Socialise

Meet new people and have a laugh! We reguarly go to the pub after games and do team activites off the football pitch, so it is a great way to make friends!

Play the game

We train twice a week and play matches every week, giving you plenty of opportunities to play the great game of football

Compete

There is nothing better than a football game on a Sunday morning. We play in a competitive league as well as take part in two cup competitions, so there are many trophies to be won! We also train twice a week which will allow you to develop as a player
