Intro to club

We are the Rose and Crown FC; a fun, friendly group of guys who play in the Berkhamstead Sunday league. We train twice a week on weekday evenings and play competitive matches against other local teams every Sunday morning. This club's ethos is all about having fun and providing an opportunity to socialise with other people and play the game we all love.

Words from the team

"My name is Charlie, the captain and founder of the Rose and Crown. I started this team 3 years ago with a small group of mates who just wanted to play football and meet new people. I've also scored some great goals which i like to remind people of ;)" - Charlie Monk, club captain
